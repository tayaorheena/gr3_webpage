Iran preparing to launch missile attack on Israel, says US, as Israel invades southern Lebanon

BBC news 10/01/2024

The US has indications that Iran is preparing to "imminently" launch a missile attack on Israel, according to several news agencies. AFP cites a US official as its source, and Reuters and Axios cite separately a senior White House official. The US official reportedly tells AFP an attack on Israel would carry "severe consequences for Iran".
